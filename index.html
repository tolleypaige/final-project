Get to Know Me!

The Freshman Life

Hey! I am Paige, a freshman at BYU! I am from a small town in Washington State called Moses Lake. I am the youngest of five BYU alumni. I earned my Associate's Degree during my last 2 years of high school. I do not quite know what I am studying. I am thinking of either business or Communication Disorders. I live in on-campus housing and have the absolute best church group! My first semester at BYU has been an adventure. Attending BYU gives students the unique opportunity to learn by study and faith. It is not the typical college experience, but it is one that I will never forget.

Roomates!

I have the coolest roommates! My roommate Cece and I knew each other from home. Amber is on BYU Colorgarud and is super good! Marina is on the women's basketball team and a super player! Nicole and Suah are so fun and creative. They are also awesome cooks! We all enjoy late-night homework sessions!

Fun

I have participated in some really fun and exciting events on campus. I have loved going to football, volleyball, soccer, and basketball games. BYU's sports teams are so good and the games are so fun! We even got a few pictures with Cosmo! I spent a weekend in Park City with RHA. We enjoy trips to Salt Lake City and surrounding areas. I have been a part of several different clubs such as swing dancing, board game club, and several fitness classes. A lot of the fun comes late at night with my roommates or friends just hanging out.

Friends

I have made lifelong friends in just this semester. All of the girls on my floor are wonderful! We have grown together this semester. I have clicked with some amazing people. We have a little friend group called The Hot Wheels! We try to do something every week, it has been a great break from schoolwork and an escape from campus. The Hot Wheels is comprised of Meliah, Adam, and I, we all like cars, hence the name. That is the amazing thing about college, you go not knowing anyone and come out having made some of your best friends. I look forward to the next 4 years and the people I will meet.
